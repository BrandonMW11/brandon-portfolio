MY GAMES

Check out my Itch.io page!

Card Quest

Card Quest is an action platformer in the same vain as Mega Man. Its defining feature is the card system where the player's stats and what tools they'll have at their disposal is determined by cards they collect in the level. The effects of these cards are randomly chosen, adding a layer of randomness to the game and a sense of replayability.

Stand Up Guy

The version of Stand Up Guy that's publically avaliable on Itch.io is a Unity remake of a C++ text-based game. In the game, you're randomly given button prompts with phrases and they have to use said phrases to put together a stand up routine. The mechanics are deeper than they may seem as whether a prompt is good or bad is determined by whether is makes sense to follow the prompt that was previously selected.

Different Directions, Same Destination

Different Directions, Same Destination is a puzzle game where the player has to move two boxes into a single goal area. The catch is that one box can only move horizontally, while the other can only move vertically, so the player has to use their brain to work around this limitation. This was made for a game jam where the theme was "polar opposites" and I used it as an oppunity to expiriment with coding different types of movement in games.
